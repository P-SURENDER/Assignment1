KAATRIN MOZHI(2018)


Viji, a passionate woman, lives a peaceful life with her husband and son but dreams of becoming an independent woman. Her life takes a turn when she gets an opportunity to become a radio jockey.

Release date: 16 November 2018 (India)
Director: Radha Mohan
