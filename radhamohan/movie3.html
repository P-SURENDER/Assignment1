BRINDAVANAM(2017)


Kannan, a hairstylist with hearing and speech impairment, is a fan of superstar Vivek. When his lifelong dream of meeting the actor comes true, they form an unusual bond between them.

Release date: 26 May 2017 (India)
Director: Radha Mohan
Music director: Vishal Chandrasekhar
Starring: Arulnithi, Vivek, Tanya
Cinematography: Vivekanand Santhosham
