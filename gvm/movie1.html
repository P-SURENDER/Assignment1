MINNALE(2001)


A man falls in love with a woman and cons his way into her heart through lies. When she finds out and confronts him, he has no sign of remorse as he feels that everything is fair in love and war.

Release date: 2 February 2001 (India)
Director: Gautham Vasudev Menon
Music director: Harris Jayaraj
Starring: Madhavan, Abbas, Reema Sen
Cinematography: R. D. Rajasekhar
