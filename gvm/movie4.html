VINNAITHAANDI VARUVAAYAA(2010)


A Hindu assistant director, Karthik, falls in love with Jessie, a Christian from a traditional family. Things change when Karthik becomes busy during a forty-day shoot in Goa.

Release date: 26 February 2010 (India)
Director: Gautham Vasudev Menon
Music director: A. R. Rahman
Starring: Surya, Sameera Reddy, Simran
Cinematography: Manoj Paramahamsa
