THAANDAVAM(2012)


Kenny, a man who is unable to see, uses echolocation to carry out an elaborate plan of revenge against the people who were responsible for a series of bomb blasts in London that killed his loved ones.

Release date: 28 September 2012
Director: A. L. Vijay
Music director: G. V. Prakash Kumar
Starring: Vikram, Anushka Shetty
Cinematography: Nirav Shah
