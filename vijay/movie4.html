THALAIVAA(2013)


Vishwa runs a small business in Sydney, unaware that his father is a gangster in Mumbai. When his father is killed by a rival, Vishwa takes over his crime syndicate with revenge on his mind.

Release date: 9 August 2013
Director: A. L. Vijay
Music director: G. V. Prakash Kumar
Starring: Vijay, Amala Paul
Cinematography: Nirav Shah
