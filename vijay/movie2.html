DEIVA THIRUMAGAL(2011)


Krishna, a man with intellectual disability, fights for the custody of his young daughter Nila. He convinces lawyer Anuradha to fight his case, when his father-in-law takes his daughter away from him.

Release date: 15 July 2011 (India)
Director: A. L. Vijay
Music director: G. V. Prakash Kumar
Starring: Vikram, Anushka Shetty
Cinematography: Nirav Shah
